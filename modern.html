Modern Representations of Medusa

The story of Medusa has been so influential throughout time, that there are numerous allusions to it, or even explicit references to it in modern culture.

In pop culture, the snake-headed terrifying image of Medusa is instantly recognizable to many. The Medusa myth has enjoyed a re-emergence since the story was featured in the "Clash of the Titans" movies which came out in 1981 and was redone in 2010. Additionally, the movie "Percy Jackson and the Olympians", which also came out in 2010, also featured the story of Medusa.



In addition to motion pictures, movies, the mythical figure of Medusa appears as a character in various TV shows, books, cartoons, and video games. Also the character has been referenced in songs, most notably by UB40, Annie Lennox, and the band Anthrax.
